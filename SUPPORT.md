## 🐛 Reporting Bugs

When reporting bugs, please include:
1. Clear description of the issue
2. Steps to reproduce
3. Expected vs actual behavior
4. Environment details (Node.js version, OS, etc.)
5. Code samples or error messages

Use our [bug report template](https://github.com/rdapify/RDAPify/issues/new?template=bug_report.md).

## ❓ FAQ

### Where do I start?
Check our [Quick Start Guide](https://rdapify.com/docs/getting-started/quick-start).

### Is RDAPify free?
Yes! RDAPify is open-source and free to use under the MIT license.

### Can I use RDAPify in production?
Yes! Many organizations use RDAPify in production. See our [Production Checklist](https://rdapify.com/docs/getting-started/production-checklist).

### How do I report a security issue?
Email security@rdapify.com. Do not create a public issue.

### Do you offer commercial support?
Yes! Contact enterprise@rdapify.com for details.
